About the app

Gabi's Tea Timer is a local-first tea timer app for saving favorite steep times, temperatures, tea icons, and preferences. No account is required.

Data and privacy

  • Tea timers, custom icons, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to add a custom icon.
  • Local notifications are used to alert you when a tea timer finishes.
  • The app does not collect, sell, or share personal data.
